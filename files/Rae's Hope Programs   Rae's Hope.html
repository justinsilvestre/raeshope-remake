Programs

 

Along with the sports and fitness activities, RAE’S HOPE provides a rich curriculum of Life Skills, Nutrition and Healthy Living, and Financial Literacy, along with Community Service activities to build balanced skills as foundations for self-reliant and fulfilling lives.   

Through practices, classroom activities, league play, workshops, field trips, and mentoring, Rae’s Hope encourages youth to create and maintain healthy lifestyles, improve themselves, and build positive relationships.

Members build their self-esteem through persistence in the face of setbacks, which leads to achievement in tackling real-world standards.  Along the way, they learn teamwork and earn a sense of belonging to a positive social community.
